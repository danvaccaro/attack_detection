attack_detection v0
By: Dan Vaccaro

A machine-learning based system for detecting attacks. Able to detect phishing attacks by being fed email text content.
Datasets from Al-Subaiey, A., Al-Thani, M., Alam, N. A., Antora, K. F., Khandakar, A., & Zaman, S. A. U. (2024, May 19). Novel Interpretable and Robust Web-based AI Platform for Phishing Email Detection. ArXiv.org. https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.11619
